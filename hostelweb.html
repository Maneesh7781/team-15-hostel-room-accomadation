ABOUT

Amity University Bengaluru, spread across a lush 70-acre campus, offers a vibrant hostel life with top-class facilities. Students enjoy a perfect blend of academic environment and extra-curricular activities. The hostels are secure, well-maintained, and foster a healthy, friendly, and collaborative atmosphere.

The university provides a safe and inclusive space for students from different backgrounds, encouraging personal growth, discipline, and community spirit. From clean rooms and hygienic food to recreational areas and peaceful green surroundings, Amity hostels are truly a home away from home.
